## <a name="interpret-events"></a><span data-ttu-id="b5109-134">解譯事件</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b5109-134">Interpret events</span></span>
<span data-ttu-id="b5109-135">StatefulRunAsyncInvocation、StatefulRunAsyncCompletion，和 StatefulRunAsyncCancellation 事件可讓服務撰寫者了解服務的生命週期--以及服務啟動、取消或完成的時機。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b5109-135">StatefulRunAsyncInvocation, StatefulRunAsyncCompletion, and StatefulRunAsyncCancellation events are useful to the service writer to understand the lifecycle of a service--as well as the timing for when a service is started, cancelled, or completed.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="b5109-136">這可能適用於偵錯服務問題或了解服務生命週期。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b5109-136">This can be useful when debugging service issues or understanding the service lifecycle.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="b5109-137">服務撰寫者應密切注意 StatefulRunAsyncSlowCancellation 及 StatefulRunAsyncFailure 事件，因為它們表示服務的問題。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="b5109-137">Service writers should pay close attention to StatefulRunAsyncSlowCancellation and StatefulRunAsyncFailure events because they indicate issues with the service.</span></span>
