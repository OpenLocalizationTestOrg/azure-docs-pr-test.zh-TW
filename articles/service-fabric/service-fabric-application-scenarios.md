<span data-ttu-id="c7406-105">Azure Service Fabric 提供可靠且彈性的平台，可讓您撰寫及執行許多類型的商務應用程式和服務。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-105">Azure Service Fabric offers a reliable and flexible platform that enables you to write and run many types of business applications and services.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c7406-106">這些應用程式和微服務可以是無狀態或具狀態，而且它們會跨虛擬機器進行資源平衡，以達到最佳效率。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-106">These applications and microservices can be stateless or stateful, and they are resource-balanced across virtual machines to maximize efficiency.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c7406-107">Service Fabric 的獨特架構可讓您在應用程式中執行接近即時的資料分析、記憶體中計算、平行交易和事件處理。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-107">The unique architecture of Service Fabric enables you to perform near real-time data analysis, in-memory computation, parallel transactions, and event processing in your applications.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c7406-108">您可以輕鬆地根據變更的資源需求上下調整 (其實是收發) 您的應用程式。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-108">You can easily scale your applications up or down (really in or out), depending on your changing resource requirements.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="c7406-109">Azure 中的 Service Fabric 平台適用於下列類別的應用程式：</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-109">The Service Fabric platform in Azure is ideal for the following categories of applications:</span></span>

* <span data-ttu-id="c7406-110">**高可用性的服務**：Service Fabric 服務藉由建立多個次要服務複本，提供快速的容錯移轉。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-110">**Highly available services**: Service Fabric services provide fast failover by creating multiple secondary service replicas.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c7406-111">如果節點、處理序或個別服務因為硬體或其他故障而停機，其中一個次要複本會升級為主要複本，以便將服務損失降到最低。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-111">If a node, process, or individual service goes down due to hardware or other failure, one of the secondary replicas is promoted to a primary replica with minimal loss of service.</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="c7406-112">**可調整的服務**：可以分割個別服務，以便在叢集中相應放大。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-112">**Scalable services**: Individual services can be partitioned, allowing for state to be scaled out across the cluster.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c7406-113">此外，可以建立並立即移除個別服務。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-113">In addition, individual services can be created and removed on the fly.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c7406-114">服務可以快速且輕鬆地由數個節點上的少數執行個體，向外延展成許多節點上的數千個執行個體，然後再次相應縮小，視資源需求而定。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-114">Services can be quickly and easily scaled out from a few instances on a few nodes to thousands of instances on many nodes, and then scaled in again, depending on your resource needs.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c7406-115">您可以使用 Service Fabric 來建置這些服務，以及管理其完整的生命週期。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-115">You can use Service Fabric to build these services and manage their complete lifecycles.</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="c7406-116">**非靜態資料的運算**：Service Fabric 可讓您建置資料、輸入/輸出，以及計算密集且具狀態的應用程式。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-116">**Computation on nonstatic data**: Service Fabric enables you to build data, input/output, and compute intensive stateful applications.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c7406-117">Service Fabric 允許在應用程式中的處理 (運算) 和資料共置。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-117">Service Fabric allows the collocation of processing (computation) and data in applications.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c7406-118">一般而言，當應用程式需要存取資料時，會發生與外部資料快取或儲存體層有關的網路延遲。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-118">Normally, when your application requires access to data, there is network latency associated with an external data cache or storage tier.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c7406-119">使用具狀態的 Service Fabric 服務可以消除這種延遲，讓更多高效能的讀取和寫入發揮功效。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-119">With stateful Service Fabric services, that latency is eliminated, enabling more performant reads and writes.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c7406-120">例如，假設您的應用程式為客戶執行近乎即時的選項建議 (來回時間低於 100 毫秒)。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-120">Say for example that you have an application that performs near real-time recommendation selections for customers, with a round-trip time requirement of less than 100 milliseconds.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c7406-121">相較於必須從遠端儲存體擷取必要資料的標準實作模型，選項建議運算與資料和規則共置的 Service Fabric 服務，其延遲和效能特性會提供使用者回應靈敏的經驗。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-121">The latency and performance characteristics of Service Fabric services (where the computation of recommendation selection is collocated with the data and rules) provides a responsive experience to the user compared with the standard implementation model of having to fetch the necessary data from remote storage.</span></span>  
* <span data-ttu-id="c7406-122">**工作階段架構的互動式應用程式**：如果您的應用程式 (例如線上遊戲或立即訊息) 需要低度延遲讀取和寫入，Service Fabric 就很有用。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-122">**Session-based interactive applications**: Service Fabric is useful if your applications, such as online gaming or instant messaging, require low latency reads and writes.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c7406-123">Service Fabric 可讓您建置這些具狀態的互動式應用程式，而不像無狀態 app 需建立不同的存放區或快取。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-123">Service Fabric enables you to build these interactive, stateful applications without having to create a separate store or cache, as required for stateless apps.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c7406-124">(這會增加延遲且可能造成一致性問題)。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-124">(This increases latency and potentially introduces consistency issues.).</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="c7406-125">**資料分析和工作流程**：Service Fabric 的快速讀取和寫入可以成為必須可靠處理事件或資料流的應用程式的依靠。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-125">**Data analytics and workflows**: The fast reads and writes of Service Fabric enable applications that must reliably process events or streams of data.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c7406-126">Service Fabric 也可讓應用程式描述處理管線，其中的結果必須能夠可靠地傳遞到下一個處理階段而不會遺失。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-126">Service Fabric also enables applications that describe processing pipelines, where results must be reliable and passed on to the next processing stage without loss.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c7406-127">這其中包括交易和財務系統，其中的資料一致性和計算保證是不可或缺。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-127">These include transactional and financial systems, where data consistency and computation guarantees are essential.</span></span>
* <span data-ttu-id="c7406-128">**資料收集、處理和 IoT**：因為 Service Fabric 透過其具狀態服務可以處理較大規模且較低延遲，所以非常適合用於數百萬個裝置上的資料處理 (這些裝置的資料和計算位於相同位置)。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-128">**Data gathering, processing and IoT**: Since Service Fabric handles large scale and has low latency through its stateful services, it is ideal for data processing on millions of devices where the data for the device and the computation are co-located.</span></span>
<span data-ttu-id="c7406-129">我們已有數名客戶使用 Service Fabric 建置 IoT 系統，包括 [BMW](https://blogs.msdn.microsoft.com/azureservicefabric/2016/08/24/service-fabric-customer-profile-bmw-technology-corporation/)、[Schneider Electric](https://blogs.msdn.microsoft.com/azureservicefabric/2016/08/05/service-fabric-customer-profile-schneider-electric/)、[Mesh Systems](https://blogs.msdn.microsoft.com/azureservicefabric/2016/06/20/service-fabric-customer-profile-mesh-systems/)。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-129">We have seen several customers who have built IoT systems using Service Fabric including [BMW](https://blogs.msdn.microsoft.com/azureservicefabric/2016/08/24/service-fabric-customer-profile-bmw-technology-corporation/), [Schneider Electric](https://blogs.msdn.microsoft.com/azureservicefabric/2016/08/05/service-fabric-customer-profile-schneider-electric/) and [Mesh Systems](https://blogs.msdn.microsoft.com/azureservicefabric/2016/06/20/service-fabric-customer-profile-mesh-systems/).</span></span>

## <a name="design-applications-composed-of-stateless-and-stateful-microservices"></a><span data-ttu-id="c7406-132">設計由無狀態與具狀態的微服務組成的應用程式</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-132">Design applications composed of stateless and stateful microservices</span></span>
<span data-ttu-id="c7406-133">使用「Azure 雲端服務」背景工作角色來建置應用程式，即是一個無狀態服務的範例。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-133">Building applications with Azure Cloud Service worker roles is an example of a stateless service.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c7406-134">相較之下，具狀態的微服務會維護要求及其回應以外的授權狀態。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-134">In contrast, stateful microservices maintain their authoritative state beyond the request and its response.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c7406-135">這可透過簡單的 API 提供高可用性和狀態的一致性，其提供受複寫支援的交易式保證。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-135">This provides high availability and consistency of the state through simple APIs that provide transactional guarantees backed by replication.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c7406-136">Service Fabric 的具狀態服務會將高可用性普及至所有類型的應用程式，而不會侷限於資料庫和其他資料存放區。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-136">Service Fabric's stateful services democratize high availability, bringing it to all types of applications, not just databases and other data stores.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c7406-137">這是自然的進展。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-137">This is a natural progression.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c7406-138">針對高可用性，應用程式已經從使用單純的關聯式資料庫進展到 NoSQL 資料庫的境界。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-138">Applications have already moved from using purely relational databases for high availability to NoSQL databases.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c7406-139">現在應用程式就能在其本身內管理它們的「熱門」狀態和資料，以便進一步提高效能，而不需犧牲可靠性、一致性或可用性。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-139">Now the applications themselves can have their "hot" state and data managed within them for additional performance gains without sacrificing reliability, consistency, or availability.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="c7406-140">建置由微服務組成的應用程式時，您通常會擁有無狀態 Web Apps (ASP.NET、Node.js 等等) 的組合，呼叫無狀態和具狀態的商務中間層服務，使用 Service Fabric 部署命令，全部部署到相同的 Service Fabric 叢集。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-140">When building applications consisting of microservices, you typically have a combination of stateless web apps (ASP.NET, Node.js, etc.) calling onto stateless and stateful business middle-tier services, all deployed into the same Service Fabric cluster using the Service Fabric deployment commands.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c7406-141">這其中的每個服務都擁有各自的可調整、可靠性和資源使用量，可大幅提升開發和生命週期管理的靈活性。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-141">Each of these services is independent with regard to scale, reliability, and resource usage, greatly improving agility in development and lifecycle management.</span></span>

<span data-ttu-id="c7406-142">具狀態的微服務簡化了應用程式設計，因此不需要傳統上為滿足純無狀態應用程式的可用性與延遲需求時所需的其他佇列與快取。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-142">Stateful microservices simplify application designs because they remove the need for the additional queues and caches that have traditionally been required to address the availability and latency requirements of purely stateless applications.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c7406-143">由於具狀態服務具有高可用性、低延遲的特性，這表示應用程式中需管理的移動組件整體上減少了。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-143">Since stateful services are naturally highly available and low latency, this means that there are fewer moving parts to manage in your application as a whole.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c7406-144">下列圖表說明設計無狀態與具狀態之應用程式間的差異。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-144">The diagrams below illustrate the differences between designing an application that is stateless and one that is stateful.</span></span> <span data-ttu-id="c7406-145">藉由運用 [Reliable Services](service-fabric-reliable-services-introduction.md) 和 [Reliable Actors](service-fabric-reliable-actors-introduction.md) 程式設計模型，具狀態服務既可降低應用程式複雜度，又可提高輸送量和降低延遲。</span><span class="sxs-lookup"><span data-stu-id="c7406-145">By taking advantage of the [Reliable Services](service-fabric-reliable-services-introduction.md) and [Reliable Actors](service-fabric-reliable-actors-introduction.md) programming models, stateful services reduce application complexity while achieving high throughput and low latency.</span></span>
